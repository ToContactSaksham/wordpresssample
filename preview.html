About Northstar Commerce

Northstar Commerce builds practical tools that help independent brands create memorable customer experiences. Its platform brings together storefront operations, reporting, and customer engagement in one focused workspace.

The company partners with growing teams that want a clear view of their business without unnecessary complexity.

With distributed teams across North America, Northstar supports thousands of merchants in bringing thoughtful products to market.
